RESEARCH IN A NUTSHELL

In Theoretical Physics, I am currently studying Gauge Theory in order to apply it to the study of Modern Field Theories. I have also studied Neutrinoless Beta Decay and conducted my Undergraduate Thesis on predicting the acoustic signatures of WIMPs in Noble Liquid detectors.

In Experimental Physics, I am currently part of the XENON Collaboration working on computational models for dark matter signal waveforms on the XENONnT detector. I have also worked on the design and development of a Dielectric Haloscope to detect Dark Photons.

In Engineering, I developed a theoretical continuous optimization algorithm based on stigmergy. I also worked on the design of haptic actuators, the theoretical modelling of soft haptic materials, and rocket payload design for a dark matter experiment.
